About

Rising from the depths of the Solana blockchain at a time when peace and fun are desperately needed. 6969 Peaceful Pigeons were created with love to bring hope and positivity to as many lives as possible.

They are here to have a good time and make the world a better place along the way. The island they call home is filled with secrets and magical beings, that can only be summoned by those with patience.

These gorgeous Pigeons aren't just awesome PFPs. They are the keys to Peaceful Island and all future utilities. Adventure and mystery await those who master inner peace.
